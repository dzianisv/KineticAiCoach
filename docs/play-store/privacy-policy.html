Privacy Policy

Kinetic AI Coach · Effective date: 20 July 2026 · Published by VibeTechnologies LLC ("AgentLabs", "we", "us")

Kinetic AI Coach ("the App") is an Android application that uses your device's camera and on-device pose detection to give you real-time feedback on exercise form, count reps and sets, and generate a personalized workout program through a conversational AI coach. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, who it is shared with, and the choices you have.

If you have questions about this policy or want to exercise any of the rights described below, contact us at support@agentlabs.cc.

Summary

  • Pose detection runs on your device. We do not receive raw video from your camera roll or gallery — the App does not request photo/media storage permission.
  • Camera frames are sent off your device only when needed to power the AI coaching feature: a pose-annotated frame montage is sent, over an encrypted connection, to our backend, which forwards it to Google's Gemini AI model to count reps and generate form tips.
  • We use Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or anonymous "guest" mode) and Cloud Firestore to let you optionally save and sync your profile and workout history across devices.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not run advertising or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs in the App.
  • You can use the App as an anonymous guest without providing your name or email at all.
  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by emailing support@agentlabs.cc.

1. Who we are

Kinetic AI Coach is developed and published by VibeTechnologies LLC, operating under the name AgentLabs. We are the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google Sign-In to create and personalize your profile. If you use Guest mode instead, no name or email is collected — Firebase issues an anonymous user ID so your in-progress session and local data still work, but nothing personally identifying is attached to it.

2.2 Camera and pose data

The App requests camera permission to power its core feature: real-time exercise form tracking. Here is exactly what happens to that camera feed:

StepWhere it happensWhat leaves your device
Live camera previewOn-deviceNothing — shown only in the app UI
Pose detection (skeleton tracking)On-device (ML Kit Pose Detection)Nothing
Red/white skeleton overlay drawn on framesOn-deviceNothing
Recent annotated frames tiled into one montage imageOn-deviceNothing yet
Montage sent for AI analysisSent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection to our Firebase Cloud Function, which authenticates your request and forwards the image to Google's Gemini model (Vertex AI)Yes — the pose-annotated image montage leaves your device for this single purpose: counting reps/sets and generating spoken technique tips

We do not store your raw camera video. The App does not request storage or media-gallery permissions, so it cannot read your existing photos or videos, and it does not save workout video files to your device or to our servers. The image montages sent for analysis are used to generate a response and are not retained by us for any purpose beyond that request.

2.3 Workout and fitness data

We store the structured results of your workouts — exercise name, reps, sets, duration, form score, and coaching feedback text — plus profile details you choose to enter such as height, weight, and fitness goals. This data is saved locally on your device (Room database) and, if you are signed in, optionally synced to Cloud Firestore so it's available if you reinstall the App or use a new device.

2.4 Chat messages

Messages you send to the in-app AI coach ("Coach Iron") are sent to Google's Gemini model (via the same encrypted backend proxy described in 2.2) to generate a response, and are stored locally so your conversation history persists between sessions.

2.5 Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect precise or approximate location.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or files.
  • We do not run advertising SDKs, ad IDs, or third-party analytics/crash-tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the core feature: analyzing your movement to count reps/sets and generate real-time, spoken form feedback.
  • To personalize your experience: generating a weekly workout program and coach responses tailored to your stated goals.
  • To let you keep your progress: syncing your profile and workout history across devices when you're signed in.
  • To operate light gamification features: streaks, XP, badges, and an optional leaderboard.
  • To respond to support requests you send to support@agentlabs.cc.

We do not use your data for advertising or to build advertising profiles.

5. Data security

All network communication between the App and our backend uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), including sign-in, Firestore sync, and the AI coaching requests described above. Access to backend infrastructure is limited to what's needed to operate the service.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Camera-frame montages sent for AI analysis are used to generate a response and are not retained beyond that request.
  • Workout history, profile, and chat data are retained locally on your device, and in Cloud Firestore if you're signed in, for as long as you keep the App installed and your account active.
  • You can delete the App at any time to remove all locally stored data from your device.
  • To request deletion of your account and any associated data stored in our backend (Firebase Authentication + Cloud Firestore), email support@agentlabs.cc from the email associated with your account (or describe your guest session if you used Guest mode). We will act on verified deletion requests within a reasonable time.

7. Your choices

  • Use the App without an account: choose Guest mode at sign-in to skip providing your name or email.
  • Camera permission: you can deny or revoke camera permission in Android Settings at any time; the AI form-tracking feature will not function without it, but other parts of the App (chat, program planning) remain usable.
  • Delete your data: see Section 6.

8. Children's privacy

Kinetic AI Coach is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@agentlabs.cc and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App or applicable law. We will update the "Effective date" above when we do. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
