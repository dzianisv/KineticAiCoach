Frequently asked questions

Is it free?

It's free to start during early access with a 3-day free trial — no credit card required upfront. After the trial, continued access to Kinetic Pro coaching features is an optional auto-renewing subscription billed through Google Play. You can cancel anytime in your Google Play account settings; the price is shown on the Play listing and inside the app before you subscribe.

Do I need equipment?

No. Just your phone and enough space to move. It works for bodyweight and home workouts — no wearables, weights, or gym required.

Does it work offline?

Pose tracking and the live skeleton overlay run on-device. AI form feedback, rep counting via Gemini, and the AI chat coach need an internet connection.

Is my camera data private?

Pose detection runs on-device. Camera frames are only sent off-device — over an encrypted connection through the app's backend to Google's Gemini model — when needed to analyze a set for rep counting and coaching feedback, for that single purpose. Guest mode requires no account at all. See our Privacy Policy for full detail.

What devices are supported?

Android 7.0 and up.
