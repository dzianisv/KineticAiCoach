How it works

Turn your camera into your coach in three steps.

1

Point & move

Prop up your phone and start your set. Kinetic runs pose detection directly on your device and draws a live skeleton overlay so you always see what the app sees.

2

AI watches your set

The annotated video is sent over an encrypted connection to the app's own Cloud Function proxy, which forwards it to Google Gemini for one purpose: counting your reps and generating specific form feedback.

3

Coach, count, improve

Kinetic reads your reps and form tips aloud in real time, logs the session, and helps you build the next workout — no advertiser ever sees your footage.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about your AI personal trainer.

Does Kinetic count reps automatically?

Yes. Kinetic runs on-device pose detection to track your joints, then sends the annotated set over an encrypted connection to Google Gemini through the app's own secure backend, which counts your sets and reps for you so you never lose count.

Is Kinetic a free AI personal trainer?

Kinetic includes a 3-day free trial. After the trial, continued access to Kinetic Pro coaching is a recurring subscription billed through Google Play. You can manage or cancel anytime in your Google Play account settings.

How does AI form correction work?

On-device machine learning overlays a live red skeleton on your camera feed. That annotated footage is analyzed by Google Gemini, which critiques your technique and returns specific, real-time form feedback that Kinetic can read aloud mid-set.

Does it work without a wearable?

Yes. Kinetic needs nothing but your phone's camera — no smartwatch, no chest strap, no gym check-in. Point your camera at yourself and train.

Is my camera data private?

Pose detection runs on-device. Camera frames leave your device only to analyze a set for rep counting and coaching, over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our backend, which forwards them to Google's Gemini model for that single purpose. Nothing is shared with advertisers, and you can use the app as an anonymous guest.
